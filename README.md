# Itinera
Elegant AI Activity Organizer that turns natural language into realistic itineraries with smart transport (Local trains, Metro), venue recommendations, plan scoring, and Google Calendar save. Built with Google ADK, Vertex AI &amp; AlloyDB.

Built on Google Cloud using **Agent Development Kit (ADK)**, **Vertex AI Gemini**, **AlloyDB**, and **MCP tools** — with strong support for Indian cities.
---

## ✨ What it does

Describe your plan in natural language:

> "Organise a lunch date at Churchgate, Mumbai this Saturday. Cute cafe vibe, budget ₹1500–2000 for two."

**Itinera** instantly delivers:
- Real venue recommendations from Google Places
- Realistic timed itinerary with transport options
- Mumbai Local trains, Metro, BEST Bus, walking & cab suggestions
- Plan scoring (vibe match, coherence, practicality)
- One-click save to Google Calendar

Works for **any activity**:
- Romantic dates
- Casual outings with friends
- Business meetings
- Family trips
- Solo explorations
